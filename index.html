Hassemer's Keyfile Builder

This tool allows you to build a 800,000-byte keyfile from a series of inputs, including a PIN, a password and the birth dates of your family members, in a deterministic manner

Building a keyfile can take up to 10 minutes, depending on your device. The whole process is executed locally on your machine. Don't close the window until the key is finished! When finished, the keyfile is either saved automatically, or a window will pop up asking for the directory where to save the file.

This tool doesn't work on some browsers, namely: Google Chrome
