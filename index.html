Hassemer's Keyfile Builder

This tool allows you to build a 800,000-byte keyfile from a series of inputs, including a PIN, a password and the birth dates of your family members, in a deterministic manner

Building a key can take up to 10 minutes, depending on your device. The whole process is executed locally in your machine. Don't close the window until the key is finished
