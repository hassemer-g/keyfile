Hassemer's Keyfile Builder

This tool allows you to build a 1,000,000-byte keyfile from a series of inputs, including a PIN, a password and the birth dates of your family members, in a deterministic manner

Building a keyfile can take a long time, up to 30 minutes or more, depending on your device. The whole process is executed locally on your machine. Don't close the tab until the key is finished! You can safely change tabs and do other things on your device while the keyfile is being built.
